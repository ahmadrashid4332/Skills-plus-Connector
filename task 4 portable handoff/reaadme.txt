# Task 4: Portable Handoff

## 📌 Handoff Method: Account Transfer
To verify the portability of the "Autonomous Career Agent" skill, I transferred the entire system prompt and configuration from my primary account to my **second account**. The goal was to ensure that the skill is not hardcoded to a specific environment and can work seamlessly for any user or on any different account.

## ⚙️ Testing & Verification
1. **Setup:** I created a new Project/Skill in my second account and pasted the exact same System Instructions (Prompt) developed in Task 1.
2. **Execution:** I uploaded a resume to the newly created skill on the second account without giving any additional text prompts.
3. **Result:** The portability was a **100% success**. The skill auto-triggered exactly as intended, performed Phase 1 (Resume Audit), and smoothly transitioned into asking for job preferences to initiate the Indeed search.
