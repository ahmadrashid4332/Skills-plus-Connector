# Skill Execution & Workflow Verification

## 📌 Overview
This document explains the step-by-step practical execution of the skill, demonstrating how it processed my resume, interacted with the live job market, and delivered a final tailored product.

## 🔄 Step-by-Step Execution Flow

### 1. Resume Upload & Auto-Audit
* **Action:** I uploaded my existing resume to the skill.
* **Result:** The skill triggered automatically. It immediately audited the CV, identified formatting flaws, corrected grammatical errors, and refined the content to look more professional.

### 2. User Interaction & Preference Gathering
* **Action:** After completing the initial audit, the AI paused and prompted me to provide my job preferences (e.g., target role and location).
* **Result:** I provided my requirements so the AI could narrow down the search.

### 3. Live Job Search & Data Fetching
* **Action:** The skill activated the **Indeed Connector** to search the live job market based on my audited resume and provided preferences.
* **Result:** The AI fetched highly relevant jobs, presented the full Job Description (JD) for the best match, and provided a **Direct Apply Link** to the job posting.

### 4. ATS-Friendly Resume Tailoring
* **Action:** Once the target job was finalized, the skill moved to the tailoring phase.
* **Result:** The AI intelligently rewrote and customized my CV according to the fetched Job Description. It naturally integrated the right keywords to ensure the resume was 100% ATS-friendly without sounding robotic.
